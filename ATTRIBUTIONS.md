## Research and Method References

The image prompts are inspired by picture-story and narrative-completion research traditions. This package does not implement official PSE, TAT, MBTI, Big Five, Jungian, clinical, or hiring scoring.

Primary image source project:

- **Images for the Picture Story Exercise (PSE)**
  OSF project: https://osf.io/pqckn/
  Project description: "This project provides a pool of commonly used and newly tested Pictures for the Picture Story Exercise."
  Picture norm table: https://osf.io/download/fxrqk/
  Norm values wiki: https://osf.io/pqckn/wiki/Norm%20values%20for%20each%20picture/

Related publication:

- Schonbrodt, F. D., Hagemeyer, B., Brandstatter, V., Czikmantori, T., Grob, M., Hennecke, M., Israel, L. S. F., Janson, K. T., Kemper, N., Koppel, L., & Schultheiss, O. C. Measuring implicit motives with the Picture Story Exercise (PSE): Databases of expert-coded German stories, pictures, and updated picture norms.
  PubMed: https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/32207995/
  OSF PDF: https://osf.io/download/jcxzu/

## Important Use Boundary

The presence of research-inspired image prompts does not make this package an official psychometric instrument. Do not present outputs as official PSE, TAT, MBTI, Big Five, Jungian, clinical, hiring, admissions, or eligibility results.
